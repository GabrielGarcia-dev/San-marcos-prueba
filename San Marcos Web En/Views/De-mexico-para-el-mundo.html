From Mexico to the World

In 1949, in Puebla, Mexico, we took the first steps in canning, laying the groundwork for our renowned canned products today. Our dedication to food quality, especially our chilies, has made us the preferred brand for all Mexican families. Behind our success is a network of collaborators committed to flavor and the highest standards. What began in the 1950s with the distribution of serrano chilies has evolved into a company with over 123 products reaching various countries. We have achieved many dreams, but our determination to bring traditional Mexican flavor to every table and kitchen, both within and outside Mexico, is stronger than ever.

We appreciate the trust that Mexican families have placed in us as their favorite brand. It is an honor to be part of their most special moments and it motivates us to continue improving. We are determined to remain the preferred choice, offering authentic Mexican flavor in each of our products. We continue to work hard to realize more dreams and extend our legacy to as many corners of the world as possible. Our goal is for our traditional Mexican flavor to make a mark across borders and delight people around the world.

Thank you for being part of our journey and for allowing us to be present in your lives. Together, we will continue to elevate the spirit of Mexican cuisine to new heights.
